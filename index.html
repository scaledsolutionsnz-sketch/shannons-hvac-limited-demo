What we do

Heating and cooling, sorted properly

Whether you're after a single heat pump, a whole-home ducted system or a service before winter, here's how we keep Central Otago homes and businesses comfortable.

01

Heat pump supply & install

Wall-mounted and floor-console heat pumps sized to your room and our climate — efficient heating through frosty winters and cooling when summer turns dry and hot.

02

Ducted central heating

Whole-home ducted systems that warm or cool every room from one quiet, zoned setup — ideal for new builds and renovations across the district.

03

Air conditioning

Cooling for homes, offices, shops and workshops — from a single high-wall head to multi-room systems, supplied and installed to handle the local heat.

04

Home ventilation

Positive-pressure and heat-transfer ventilation to clear condensation, damp and stale air — keeping your home drier, healthier and easier to heat.

05

Servicing & repairs

Filter cleans, regas, fault-finding and tune-ups that keep your heat pump running efficiently and your warranty intact — for any brand.

06

Light commercial systems

Heating and cooling for shops, offices, cafés and workshops, plus ongoing maintenance to keep them running through the busy season.

About us
