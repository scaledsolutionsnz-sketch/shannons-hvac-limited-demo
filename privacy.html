Privacy Policy

This is a demonstration website. The policy below is a standard placeholder and should be reviewed before the site goes live.

1. What we collect

When you submit an enquiry or feedback form, we collect the details you provide — your name, contact details, suburb and message — so we can respond and quote your job.

2. How we use it

We use your information only to reply to your enquiry, arrange work and keep in touch about that job. We don't use it for anything you haven't asked us to.

3. Sharing

We don't sell or trade your information. We only share it where needed to carry out the work you've requested or where required by law.

4. Keeping it safe

Form submissions are handled through a secure third-party form service. We keep your details only as long as needed to help you.

5. Contact

For any privacy questions, call or text Shannon on 027 444 6761.
